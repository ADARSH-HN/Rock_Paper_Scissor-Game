Rules Of The Game

How to Play

• The game has 3 rounds.

• Choose Rock, Paper, or Scissors each round.

• The computer will choose at the same time.

Winning Rules:

Rock beats > Scissors

Scissors beats > Paper

Paper beats > Rock

• Winning a round gives you 1 point.

• After 3 rounds, the player with the highest score wins.

• If both scores are equal, the game ends in a draw.

Good luck!
